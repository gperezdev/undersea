ABOUT US

UNDERSEA is a diving center and diving school located in the heart of the Costa Brava (Girona).
We offer unforgettable courses and outings taking care of the safety, professionalism and quality of our services.
We have a team of experienced diving instructors and guides who will make you enjoy diving in a comfortable, fun and safe way.

We apply environmental criteria, such as waste management and the reduction of energy consumptions.

We don't put pressure on animals to get them to come out of ther hiding places.

We use mooring buoys, whenever possible, with ecological anchors.

We do eco-briefings.

IMMERSIONS

Do you want to free dive through a sunken ship from World War II?
Located off the coast of Palamós, the 40-meter-long ship rests at a depth of 31 meters.
This old ship, used as a tugboat for the German Navy during World War II and a merchant transporter after the war, was requisitioned for drug trafficking.
After spending three years in the port of Palamós, the ship was sunk just off the coast to encourage sport diving.

DISCOVERY

Enjoy the experience of discovering the wonders of the underwater world!
The diving consists of a short theoretical explanation about diving (safety, equipment, etc.) followed by a dive from a boat led by a monitor to a maximum depth of 7 meters for about 50 minutes.
Once the dive is finished, the boat goes to the port, then transfer to the diving center, desalination, shower and end of the activity.
The entire activity lasts approximately 3 hours.
